How to Book Bus Tickets Online on FastX

  1. Enter the Origin city and Destination city details in the “From” and “Station” fields respectively
  2. Select the “Journey date” from calendar icon and click on the “Search” button
  3. Select any bus of your choice from the list of buses which are displayed
  4. Check the Booking ticket information
  5. Select a seat “Continue to payment”
  6. Complete the payment process by selecting your preferred mode of payment
  7. Once the payment process is done, you will receive an M-ticket on your mobile via SMS, WhatsApp message and an e-ticket on email ID provided at “Enter Contact Details” field.
